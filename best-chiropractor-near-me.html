Frequently Asked Questions

Why chiropractic adjustments don't hold?

Chiropractic adjustments may not hold if underlying issues such as poor posture, muscle imbalances, or lifestyle habits contribute to recurrent misalignments. Addressing these factors through complementary therapies, exercise, and ergonomic adjustments may help improve the longevity of chiropractic adjustments.

Should you get a massage right after chiropractor?

Combining chiropractic care with massage therapy can be beneficial for some individuals, as both modalities can address different aspects of musculoskeletal health. However, it's essential to consult with your chiropractor and massage therapist to ensure coordination of care and avoid overstimulation of muscles.

Can I wear a bra to the chiropractor?

Yes, you can wear a bra to the chiropractor. However, it's advisable to wear comfortable clothing that allows the chiropractor easy access to the areas needing adjustment.

Should I stretch before chiropractor?

Stretching before a chiropractic session may help improve flexibility and prepare your muscles for adjustments. However, it's essential to perform gentle stretches and avoid overexertion to prevent injury.
