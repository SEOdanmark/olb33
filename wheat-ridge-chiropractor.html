Frequently Asked Questions

Should I rest after chiropractic adjustment?

Resting after a chiropractic adjustment can help your body integrate the changes and promote healing. However, light activities such as walking or gentle stretching may also be beneficial to prevent stiffness and promote circulation.

What should you not do after seeing a chiropractor?

Avoid intense physical activities immediately after a chiropractic session. Refrain from lifting heavy objects or engaging in strenuous exercises that may strain your muscles and undo the adjustments.

Why do people not respect chiropractors?

Some people may not respect chiropractors due to misconceptions about their qualifications, concerns about safety and effectiveness, or negative experiences with specific practitioners. It's essential to research and choose a qualified and reputable chiropractor for optimal results.

When should chiropractic not be used?

Chiropractic care may not be suitable for individuals with certain medical conditions such as fractures, severe osteoporosis, or spinal infections. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional to determine if chiropractic care is appropriate for your specific situation.
